Email Marketing Planning Template

Use this template to strategically organize your email marketing. Available for Microsoft Excel and Google Sheets.

Use this template to:
  • Organize which emails you send to each segments of your database
  • Remember what A/B tests you're conducting
  • Summarize high-level results
  • Gather data on individual email performance
  • Compile data on Excel or Google Sheets
  • Automatically calculate delivery, open, and click through rates

Planning your email marketing schedule doesn't have to be stressful.
